Ascent Physiotherapy Services

Ascent Physiotherapy is a full service physiotherapy clinic. We offer a wide range of services to meet individual needs and take pride in making customized plans for everyone.

We offer:

  • Physical Therapy
  • Manual and Manipulative Therapy
  • Intramuscular Stimulation (IMS)
  • Acupuncture
  • Radial Shockwave Therapy
  • Personal Training
  • Worksafe BC
  • ICBC
  • Treatment for TMJ Dysfunction
  • Myofascial Release and Craniosacral Therapy
  • Sports Therapy
  • Athletic Taping/ Bracing

Specialties

Physical Therapy

Experience personalized rehabilitation programs designed to restore mobility, alleviate pain, and enhance overall function, guided by our expert physical therapists.

Intramuscular Stimulation (IMS)

Discover relief from chronic muscle pain through IMS, a precise technique targeting trigger points to release tension and promote healing.

Acupuncture

Harness the power of ancient healing with acupuncture, a holistic approach that stimulates key points in the body to reduce pain, improve circulation, and enhance well-being.

Radial Shockwave Therapy

Accelerate healing and tissue repair with radial shockwave therapy, a non-invasive treatment that promotes regeneration and alleviates musculoskeletal discomfort.

Personal Training

Elevate your fitness journey with personalized training programs, tailored to your unique needs and goals, under the guidance of our experienced fitness professionals.

TMJ Dysfunction

Address temporomandibular joint issues effectively through specialized therapies, relieving discomfort and restoring proper function for improved jaw health.

Myofascial Release and Craniosacral Therapy

Experience gentle yet effective techniques targeting fascial and craniosacral restrictions, promoting relaxation, and facilitating the body's natural healing processes.

Sports Therapy

Optimize athletic performance and prevent injuries with our sports therapy services, combining advanced techniques to enhance strength, flexibility, and overall athletic well-being.
