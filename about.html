Our Team

Kendra Mulligan BScPT, CGIMS, FCAMPT

Kendra, a University of Alberta graduate, spent 18 years in Northern Alberta, including five as a physical therapy practice owner. Raised on a BC cattle farm, she's active in mountain biking and sports. In 2013, she earned FCAMPT certification and has since contributed to physiotherapy education. Co-owner of Ascent Physiotherapy in Comox since 2010, Kendra emphasizes evidence-based manual therapy. In 2021, she added Anatomical Acupuncture to her certifications.

Trish Leslie BScPT

Trish, a 2011 honours graduate from Queen Margaret University in Edinburgh, has diverse experience in orthopaedics, women’s health, geriatrics, neurology, and sports physiotherapy. She's worked with rugby players in Edinburgh and Victoria, and since 2012, has been active in private practice and acute care. Trish specializes in manual therapies, physiotherapy modalities, and exercise prescription. A Ucluelet native, she's now thriving in the Comox Valley and continues to enhance her skills with additional courses in orthopaedics and women’s health, along with certification in acupuncture from Acupuncture Foundation Canada Institute.

Jared Hromika MPT, FDN/IMS

Jared, a 2014 University of British Columbia Masters of Physical Therapy graduate, moved to the Comox Valley in 2015 with his wife. Before relocating, he gained diverse experience in Vancouver through locum positions in private practice orthopaedics. Jared's treatment approach includes movement assessment, manual therapy, and exercise prescription to enhance movement and alleviate pain. He's trained in Neurokinetic Therapy, addressing compensation patterns. Jared offers bike fitting services, combining his triathlon passion and UBC training in rider assessment. Beyond the clinic, he enjoys swimming, trail running, softball, hockey, and optimizing bike performance.

Shadi Fleifel MPT, FDN/IMS

Shadi, a key member of the Ascent Physiotherapy Team since 2018, holds a Kinesiology degree from Western University and a Master’s in Physiotherapy from Perth, Australia (2008). With over 10 years of clinical experience, he specializes in sports rehabilitation, notably with premier rugby clubs and the Canada Rugby 7's team. Shadi excels in treating sports and everyday injuries, continuously advancing his skills through courses like Brian Mulligan mobilization and functional fascial taping. Outside work, he enjoys family time, crossfit, swimming, biking, running, rugby, basketball, and surfing.

Chelsey de la Rey MPT

Chelsey, with a Trinity Western University degree in Human Kinetics and a 2019 Masters of Physical Therapy graduate from UBC, offers personalized care in the Comox Valley. Certified in Orthopedic Division Level 1, she focuses on manual therapy, exercise prescription, and self-management education. Actively pursuing further education, she's interested in manual therapy, pain science, and active rehabilitation. Outside work, Chelsey and her husband enjoy golf, fishing, and various sports.

Personal Training

Carter Mulligan

Carter, a Registered BCRPA Fitness Leader with certifications in Personal Training, Weight Training, and Fitness Theory, adheres to the national standard for fitness leadership. With a personal focus on Bodybuilding, he has dedicated three years to extensive training. Carter, a former competitive lacrosse and rugby athlete, brings over a year of experience in the rehabilitation setting. In this role, he assists veterans, injured individuals, and weekend warriors in achieving their fitness, strength, and functional goals.
